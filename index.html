Crystal Collectorby Michael Wolf

A few basic rules:
  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below: amethyst, diamond, emerald and quartz. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to "your number".

  2. By matching "your number" to the "number to match", you win! If "your number" goes above the "number to match", you lose. Winning or losing ends the round.

  3. The value of each crystal is unkown until clicked. At the start of every round, the crystals will have a new value. So if you thought you could simply memorize the four numbers matched to each crystal, sorry to disappoint.

Good Luck!
